MY FAVORITE MOVIES

  • The day of the Jackal

    • I admire how the Jackal is calm, precise, and highly professional,and how the film shows every step of his
    mission with realistic detail. At the same time,the French police’s investigation keeps you on edge,
    making you feel like you’re part of the chase.

  • The beekeeper

    • Jason Statham plays Adam Clay, a quiet, reclusive man who lives a simple life as a beekeeper on a rural farm.
    But when a close friend falls victim to a cruel phishing scam that wipes out her life savings, Adam goes on a
    relentless mission of revenge.

  • The Expendables

    • I love The Expendables because it brings together some of the greatest action legends from different eras
    into one explosive team.The movie mixes old-school action with modern intensity full of big fights,
    brotherhood, loyalty, and teamwork.
